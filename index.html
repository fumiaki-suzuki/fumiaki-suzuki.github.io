About Me (cv)

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Leibniz University Hannover funded by the ERC Starting Grant "RationAlgic". My mentor is Stefan Schreieder.

Before that, I was a Hedrick Assistant Adjunct Professor at the University of California, Los Angeles under the direction of Burt Totaro.

I completed my PhD at the University of Illinois at Chicago in 2020 under the supervision of Lawrence Ein.

Research Interests

I am interested in algebraic geometry, especially in algebraic cycles, birational geometry, and Hodge theory.
