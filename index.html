About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Leibniz University Hannover funded by the ERC Starting Grant "RationAlgic". My mentor is Stefan Schreieder. Before that, I was a Hedrick Assistant Adjunct Professor at UCLA under the direction of Burt Totaro. I completed my PhD at UIC in 2020 under the supervision of Lawrence Ein.

I am interested in algebraic geometry, especially in algebraic cycles, birational geometry, and Hodge theory.
